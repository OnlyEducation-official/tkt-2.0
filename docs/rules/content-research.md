KeralaвАЩs most comfortable broadly reliable season is still the cooler tourism window from roughly October/November through February, while the southwest monsoon normally reaches Kerala around 1 June and the state also receives a second rainy phase in October/November. Hill stations such as Munnar and Thekkady are cooler than the coast, while backwaters and Kochi remain more humid.
